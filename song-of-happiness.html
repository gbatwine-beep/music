A. Song of Happiness (歡樂歌)

"Song of Happiness" is a lively and joyful piece. It is often sung at celebrations and gatherings. It conveys themes of friendship（友谊）, unity（团结）, and optimism（乐观）.

With its upbeat melody and simple, memorable lyrics, the song encourages everyone to sing together and share joyful moments in life. Not only does it showcase the deep emotions between people, but it also reflects a vision of a harmonious and happy society, serving as a wonderful expression of joy and gratitude.

Values to be learnt from this Chinese piece:

  • Friendship
  • Unity
  • Harmony
  • Gratitude
