Beijing 北京

Beijing, the capital of China, is renowned for its imperial court music（宫廷音乐） and Peking opera（京剧） traditions. As a cultural and political center for centuries, Beijing has preserved and developed unique musical forms that reflect its historical significance.

The city's musical heritage includes both the refined "Yayue"（雅乐） ceremonial music of ancient courts and the vibrant, expressive sounds of Peking opera, which combines music, vocal performance, mime, dance, and acrobatics. Beijing's music represents the formal, structured side of Chinese musical tradition.
