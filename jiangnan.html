Jiangnan 江南

Jiangnan Sizhu（江南丝竹） is a traditional form of folk music from the Jiangsu and Zhejiang regions of China, characterized by its rich cultural heritage and unique regional features. This music typically employs silk and bamboo instruments, including the erhu（二胡）, guzheng（古筝）, dizi（笛子）, and suona（唢呐）, creating a gentle and beautiful playing style often accompanied by flowing melodies and rhythms.
